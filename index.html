About Us




Mind Hive in a nutshell


Mind Hive has been created to provide access to resources and study material to students.

We believe that every one has the right to education and are here to make it less strenous and affordable for all.


Some enjoy flipping through pages, others prefer getting all the info they need, at the tap of a button and here, we do just that.

In the present day and age everything is being made easy, education should be too, and with us, learning happens with the click of a button.


We provide a complete curriculum of several subjects including exercices, past exam papers, case studies, external resources to ensure that getting A's, distinctions, passing with flying colors is possible for all
