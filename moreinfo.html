Mind Hive in a nutshell

Mind Hive is an online study material platform designed for students to make learning less stressful then it really needs to be. Here we believe that learning can be fun and that everybody can pass given the right resources, so we aim to provide you with those and so much more to make your academic journey less strenous and that just at the tap of a button!


No body should need to hit the library to study when a click on their cell can do the same thing, especially when education is still a luxury in various parts of the world. Mind Hive wants to make it affordable for you, your children or students.

Our courses

At Mind Hive we believe that access to information is key to better lives in various aspects and considering that technology is really advancing fast we believe that learners with related interest should be provided with what there is to know about the digital world we are living in, for this reason we have created a course to introduce young minds to the world of technology


The course provides a basic overview of what the internet is and touches on its various aspects as well as an introduction to the world wide web and various of its facets

At a more advanced level we introduce a web development and design course to help the intrerested student get started at a fairly young age to not struggle at tertiary education levels
